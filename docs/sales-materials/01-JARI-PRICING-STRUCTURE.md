## PRODUCT CARDS (One-Time Purchase)

Cards are **slots** for your products. Buy once, keep forever.

### Card Bundles

| Bundle | Total Cards | Price | Per Card |
|--------|-------------|-------|----------|
| **Included** | 3 | FREE | - |
| **Starter Pack** | 7 (+4) | KES 350 | KES 87 |
| **Growth Pack** | 10 (+7) | KES 550 | KES 79 |
| **Pro Pack** | 15 (+12) | KES 850 | KES 71 |

### Scaling Beyond 15 Cards

| Card Range | Additional Monthly Fee |
|------------|----------------------|
| 1-15 cards | No extra fee |
| 16-30 cards | +KES 200/month |
| 31-60 cards | +KES 500/month |
| 60+ cards | Enterprise (Contact us) |

**To add cards beyond 15:**
- Purchase another bundle (e.g., Pro Pack for +15 cards = 30 total)
- Monthly subscription adjusts based on active card count

## THEMES (One-Time Unlock)

Themes unlock **checkout styles** optimized for different business types.

| Theme | Price | Best For | Checkout Style |
|-------|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Quick Sell** | KES 500 | Simple products, impulse buys | Minimal steps, fast checkout |
| **Visual Menu** | KES 600 | Food, bakery, restaurants | Grid display, cart checkout |
| **Deep Dive** | KES 800 | Premium, complex products | Gallery, specs, storytelling |
| **Events/Booking** | KES 1,000 | Classes, appointments, tickets | Date/time picker, capacity |
| **Services** | KES 800 | Coaching, consulting, freelance | Packages, inquiry flow |
| **Catalog** | KES 400 | Browse-only, WhatsApp inquiry | No direct checkout |

### Free Theme Policy

**Your first theme is FREE** - the one that matches your business type selected during signup.

- Signup as bakery → Visual Menu FREE
- Signup as coach → Events/Booking FREE
- Signup as reseller → Quick Sell FREE

**Additional themes** purchased separately to expand your offerings.
