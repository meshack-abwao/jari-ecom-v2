**Don't pitch immediately.** Ask questions to understand their JOB:

> *"Tell me about your business - what do you sell and how do customers usually find you?"*

### Pain Discovery
> *"Walk me through what happens when someone wants to buy from you..."*

- Manual DM conversations
- Payment confirmation hassles
- Lost sales / slow responses
- No way to track what's working

> *"How many sales do you think you lose because of this process?"*

> *"What would it mean for your business if every interested customer could pay in 30 seconds?"*
